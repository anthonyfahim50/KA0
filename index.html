Who We Are

A sisterhood built on purpose, principle, and people

Founded in 1870 as the first Greek-letter fraternity known among women, Kappa Alpha Theta exists to give women the widest opportunity for intellectual and personal growth. At Eta Omega, that founding vision shapes everything we do at Saint Louis University.

Theta for CASA

Our national philanthropic partner

Court Appointed Special Advocates (CASA) are trained, everyday volunteers appointed by judges to speak up for children who have experienced abuse or neglect. A CASA volunteer is often the one steady, caring adult in a child's life as they move through the court and foster-care systems.

Kappa Alpha Theta has proudly supported CASA as its national philanthropy since 1989. Every dollar Eta Omega raises helps ensure that more children have a voice and a safe, permanent place to call home.
